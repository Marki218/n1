CORE VALUES

(PICA)
👥

People-Centered

We are people-centered. We prioritize the needs and well- being of the people we serve.

⚖️

Integrity

We value integrity. Whatever we do, we do it with honesty, fairness, and consistency.

❤️

Compassion

We have compassion. We are sympathetic for patients who are suffering from chronic kidney disease. We desire to alleviate their suffering.

📊

Accountability

We act with accountability. We take ownership of our actions and are accountable for their outcomes.

Our Mission

The mission of 1 PAG-ASA Kidneys Alagaan Natin is to promote awareness about kidney health and encourage proactive steps to prevent kidney diseases.

Through education, community outreach, and health advocacy programs, we inform people about the importance of proper hydration, balanced nutrition, regular medical checkups, and healthy lifestyle choices.

By spreading knowledge and providing support, 1 PAG-ASA aims to reduce the risk of kidney disease and help individuals live healthier, informed lives.

Our Inspiration

Mr. Vincent Papa, a kidney survivor and chronic kidney disease patient, inspired the creation of 1-PAG-ASA Kidneys Alagaan Natin Corp.

After more than three years of dialysis, he experienced firsthand the physical, emotional, and financial challenges faced by many kidney patients. This motivated him to help others through education, early detection, and community awareness programs.

Today, 1 PAG-ASA continues to serve as a platform for spreading knowledge, encouraging healthy lifestyles, and giving hope to individuals and families affected by kidney disease.

Our Vision

1 PAG-ASA envisions a healthier Philippines where individuals are fully aware of kidney health, empowered to take preventive actions, and have access to proper medical care.

Through continuous education, early detection programs, and strong community partnerships, we aim to reduce kidney disease cases and improve the quality of life for every Filipino.

We continue to inspire hope, compassion, and action through community outreach, free awareness programs, and public health advocacy.

Our Mission

Our mission is to educate communities about kidney disease prevention, promote healthy lifestyles, provide support to kidney patients, and strengthen partnerships with healthcare providers and volunteers.

Our Advocacy Programs

💧 Hydration Campaign

Encouraging people to drink enough water every day to support kidney function and remove toxins from the body.

🥗 Healthy Lifestyle Program

Promoting balanced diets, low salt intake, and healthy living habits to reduce the risk of kidney disease.

🏥 Free Health Screening

Encouraging communities to participate in early kidney disease detection through regular health checkups.

📚 Health Education

Providing seminars and educational materials to increase public awareness about kidney health.

👨‍👩‍👧 Community Outreach

Working with communities to spread awareness and provide support for healthy living practices.

🌍 Awareness Campaign

Using posters, campaigns, and events to promote kidney health awareness across schools and communities.
